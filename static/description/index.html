Force Delete Multiple | Invoice/Credit Note For Odoo 11

Powered by AhmedHassan

Overview :
  • Force Delete Multiple | requires cancel_invoice to Allow Cancelling Entries
  • Force Delete Multiple | is able to delete all invoices & CreditNote [draft or open or cancel or paid]
  • if invoice is paid or open (and have any payment)
  • The payment will not be deleted
  • but the remove match with payment (reconcile)
  • and Customer Still Have this payment
  • if invoice is open and already matched with Credit Note , the invoice and CreditNote will be deleted
  • if CreditNote is open or paid and already matched with invoice , the invoice and CreditNote will be deleted
  • Be careful, you will not be able to recover anything deleted
  • Only allowed to appear to (Administration / Access Rights) group

Privacy :
  • Only allowed to appear to (Administration / Access Rights) group
How to find :
  • Go to on invoices list
  • 1.Select Some Invoices or CreditNote
  • 2.click to > Actions
  • 3.Choose Action force Delete Multiple (Delete action or just cancel)
